The Love Letter

Polar coordinates (极坐标系): $r(t)=1+\cos(t)$

Parameter Equation(参数方程)

$x(t)=(1-\sin(t))\cos(t) $

$y(t)=(1-\sin(t))\sin(t) $

Coffee (咖啡拉花)

$x(t)=(1-\sin(t))\cos(t)(1+t) $

$y(t)=((1-\sin(t))\sin(t)+\frac{3}{4})(1+t) $

Balloon (气球)

$x(s,t)= (1-\sin(t))\cos(t)\sin(s) $

$y(s,t)=(1-\sin(t))\sin(t)\sin(s) $

$z(s,t)=\cos(s) $

Torus (平环)

$x(s,t)= (1-\sin(t))\cos(t)(\sin(s)+2) $

$y(s,t)=((1-\sin(t))\sin(t)+\frac{1}{2})(\sin(s)+2) $

$z(s,t)=\cos(s) $

Stairs (心路阶梯)

$x(s,t)= (1-\sin(t))\cos(t)t(1.1^s) $

$y(s,t)=((1-\sin(t))\sin(t)+\frac{3}{4})t(1.1^s) $

$z(s,t)=-1.1^s $
